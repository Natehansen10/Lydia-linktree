Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Lydia Hansen Creator Analytics application ("the App"), operated by Hansen Creator Tools ("we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, and protects data.

1. Who This Applies To

The App is a private tool used by a single, named TikTok creator account holder. It is not available to the general public, and this policy applies only to data belonging to that authorized account holder.

2. Data We Collect

Through TikTok's Login Kit and Display API, and only after explicit authorization by the account holder, the App retrieves:

  • Basic profile information (display name, avatar)
  • Video metadata (titles, publish dates, view counts)
  • Engagement metrics (likes, comment counts, and comment content)

We do not collect data belonging to any TikTok account other than the authorized account holder's own account.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used to power a private analytics dashboard for the account holder's own review, supporting content strategy decisions and brand partnership reporting prepared by the account holder.

4. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and retrieved data are stored securely and are not accessible to any party other than the account holder and the individual(s) operating the App on her behalf.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share data with third parties. Summary metrics may be included by the account holder in materials she personally prepares for brand partners; this is performed manually by the account holder and is not an automated data-sharing function of the App.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed to support the App's function. The account holder may request deletion of all stored data at any time by contacting us, and may revoke the App's access to her TikTok account at any time through TikTok's account settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to lydia@idolsandicons.co.
